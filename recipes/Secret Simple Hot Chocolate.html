Secret Simple Hot Chocolate

I was experimenting in the kitchen with milk and sugar, when suddenly the idea of chocolate came to my mind. I looked in the cabinet and saw a fresh bottle of chocolate syrup. I added it to my hot cup of milk and sugar and ta-da! Secret simple hot chocolate! Add marshmallows, whipped cream, or Cool WhipÂ® to make even tastier.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons chocolate syrup

Directions

  1. Stir milk and sugar together in a microwave-safe glass or mug. Microwave until hot, about 2 minutes. Stir in chocolate syrup until completely incorporated.
